How to search Chinese authors on NASA/ADS system

Published: 2023-02-10 (Fri)

In blog.

You may have encountered this problem. It is really hard to search Chinese authors (and maybe similar problems for people from some other cultures).

Luckily, a minor trick can help a lot. Add a "=" symbol will enforce the system to search the papers with exact match.

For example, to search my papers on ADS

=author:"Li, Zhaozhou" OR =author:"Li, Zhao-Zhou"

I publish papers at early times as "Li, Zhao-Zhou", later I decide to simplify it as "Li, Zhaozhou" as that is what I use in the daily life.
